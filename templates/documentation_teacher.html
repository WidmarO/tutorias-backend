Welcome to Documentation for the Teacher

End point: 
 /teacher/cod_teacher

Method : GET

This endpoint get a teachers from the DB

                            Authorization: Bearer eyJ0eXAiOiJKV1QiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9.....eyJ0eXAiOiJKV1QiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9
